FRP Manhole Covers

Complies with EN 124:2015. Highly durable, anti-slip textured, and available from 2.5 Tons to 40 Tons loading rates.

Heavy-Duty Composite Covers

Engineered to replace traditional metal and concrete covers with zero threat of theft.

TOPAZ FRP manhole covers are manufactured using state-of-the-art high-pressure hydraulic compression molding technology. By aligning reinforcing glass fibers with UV-stabilized polymer matrices, we produce covers with high impact tolerance, minimal thermal expansion, and resistance to sewage chemicals.

We offer fully customized designs where client logos and specific text (e.g. "WATER", "Sewerage", "Electrical") can be embossed directly into the covers.

EN 124 Loading Class Guide

We manufacture covers matching all standard vehicle loads specified in global standards.

Group Load Class Topaz Code Proof Load Recommended Traffic Application Description
Group 1 A15 ELD 2.5T 15 KN (2.5 Tons) Areas which can only be used by pedestrians and pedal cyclists.
Group 2 B125 LD 5T / MD 12.5T 125 KN (12.5 Tons) Suitable for use in light vehicle movement, domestic driveways, and footways.
Group 3 C250 HD 25T 250 KN (25 Tons) Suitable for use in service lanes, car parking lots, gully tops, and curb sides.
Group 4 D400 EHD 40T 400 KN (40 Tons) Carriageways of roads, hard shoulders, and highway parking areas for all vehicles.
Group 5 E600 E600 600 KN (60 Tons) Areas imposing high wheel loads, e.g., docks, freight terminals, and aircraft hangars.
Group 6 F900 F900 900 KN (90 Tons) Special high-stress areas like aircraft runways and military deployment bases.
