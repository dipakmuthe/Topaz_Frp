Engineering Advantages

Why Choose TOPAZ FRP?

Fibre-Reinforced Polymer (FRP) represents the pinnacle of modern material design, offering significant advantages over cast iron and concrete.

Zero Scrap Value

FRP is non-reprocessible and has no resale scrap value, making it totally unattractive to thieves, reducing theft and accident risks.

45% Lighter Weight

Provides incredible strength while being 45% lighter than cast iron. A single worker can easily install and handle it without injury risk.

Corrosion Free

Completely resistant to chemical sewer gases, sewer water, acidic rain, and weathering. Does not rust, warp, or crumble.

High Load Capacity

Exceeds load ratings for EN 124:1994 / EN 124:2015 standards from light A15 loads (2.5T) to heavy D400 carriageway loads (40T).

Technical Comparison

TOPAZ FRP vs Cast Iron (CI / DI)

Why engineers and developers worldwide are replacing cast iron with composite materials.

Feature / Specification TOPAZ FRP Covers Cast Iron (CI / DI) Covers
Theft Protection 100% Anti-Theft (Zero scrap resale value) Highly prone to theft (High scrap resale value)
Service Life Min. 30 Years (Does not corrode or rust) Low service life (Corrodes heavily with sewer gas)
Weight & Handling 45% Lighter (Single worker can install safely) Extremely Heavy (Requires multiple workers, high injury risk)
Corrosion & Chemicals Immune to sewage water, rain, acids Gets rusted & structurally weak due to sewer chemicals
Painting & Maintenance Self-pigmented, UV-stable (No painting needed) Requires regular, expensive epoxy painting
Locking / Pressure Integrated locking limits displacement from pressure Prone to popping open from low-lying pressure

Quality Standards

Tested for Excellence

We are the only manufacturer in the industry performing GC testing on raw resins.

Our covers are tested for load-bearing capacity and permanent set deformation as per **BSEN 124:1994/2015** and **IS 1726** parameters.

We perform standard Gas Chromatography (GC) on resins, Tensile Mat tests for raw fiber layout, and post-production hydraulic load trials to certify every batch before dispatch.
