FRP Recessed Covers

Designed to conceal inspection chambers in high-value aesthetic paved and landscaped areas.

Aesthetic Concealment

Integrates interlocking blocks or stone tiles directly into the cover.

Recessed manhole covers are utilized within paved areas in order to hide inspection chambers, manholes, and access fittings. When aesthetic value is required, Recessed manhole covers are vital when compared with regular manhole covers normally employed in modern construction.

The cover features a tray (either 65mm or 32mm depth) into which block pavers, granite tiles, concrete, or sod/grass can be filled. The outer metal edge is practically invisible once filled, preserving the clean look of pathways, gardens, and premium building entrance plazas.

Example of cover integrated flush with surrounding block paved pathways
