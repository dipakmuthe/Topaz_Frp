Landscaping Pots

FRP Designer Planters

Weather-proof pots for corporate offices, malls, hotels, and luxury penthouses.

Unlike clay pots that break easily or concrete planters that are too heavy to relocate, TOPAZ FRP designer planters offer the perfect combination of structural strength and portability.

Available in cylindrical, square, custom geometric shapes, and a wide array of premium architectural shades. Unaffected by constant soil moisture, fertilizer salts, and intense solar UV heat.

We custom-make sizes as per your exact landscaping requirements.

Outdoor Structures

FRP Pergolas

Elegant garden and terrace pavilions with wood-like textures.

FRP pergolas offer a premium corporate appearance for patio seating, seaside resorts, and luxury residential rooftops. They mimic high-quality teak wood structures but do not rot, peel, warp, or catch termites.

Completely immune to coastal salty air, acid rain, and fungal weathering. Highly durable and zero maintenance required.
