FRP Gully Gratings

High-water drainage flow capacity coupled with extreme vehicle load resistance. Available with frame or only grating.

High Drainage Solutions

FRP Drainage Gratings

Designed for stronger and easier drainage solutions in heavy automotive areas.

TOPAZ FRP Gully Gratings are engineered specifically for high-capacity stormwater runoff management. By replacing traditional cast-iron grates, municipalities and corporate builders achieve robust loading strengths while eliminating theft risks entirely.

Withstand Heavy Vehicle Load

Engineered for parking zones and carriageway borders (up to 40T loading).

High Corrosion Resistance

100% unaffected by road salts, wastewater chemicals, or grease.

Zero Thermal Expansion

Will not bind in frames or buckle during extreme hot summer months.

Zero Scrap Value

Completely eliminates gully cover thefts, avoiding open drain hazards.
