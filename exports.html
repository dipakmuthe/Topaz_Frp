Our Logistics Flow

Seamless 4-Step Export Process

How we manage corporate inquiries from custom design parameters to global port arrival.

Step 1

Inquiry & Technical CAD Drawings

Our team evaluates your project specifications. We prepare customized load configuration estimates, match color request pigmentation, and provide official CAD drawings for review within 24 hours.

Step 2

Precision Manufacturing

Once approved, composite products are molded at our state-of-the-art facility in Pune. Using high-pressure hydraulic molding, we ensure precise resin-to-fiber integration with zero internal air pockets.

Step 3

Stringent Batch Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes strict quality compliance audits. We perform Gas Chromatography (GC) on resins, tensile strength layouts, and hydraulic testing as per global BS EN 124 standards, issuing certified reports.

Step 4

Palletized Global Delivery

Covers are packed on reinforced pallets, shrink-wrapped, and strapped. We manage container transport to Nhava Sheva (JNPT) port in Mumbai and organize maritime ocean freight routes to your destination harbor.
