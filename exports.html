Export Packaging Standards

Ensuring materials arrive in perfect condition after ocean shipping.

High humidity inside containers can cause traditional materials to oxidize. TOPAZ FRP composite products are completely immune to humidity. To avoid mechanical abrasions and chips during shipping transit, we pack covers using heavy-duty plastic shrink wrap, layering them on reinforced wooden pallets strapped with high-tensile steel bands.

This guarantees that external paint textures, embossing features, and structural seals arrive in factory-fresh condition.

Port Logistics Checklist:

  • Palletized cargo optimization for easy forklift handling.
  • Standard packing slips and custom HS Code documentation.
  • Quick custom clears handling through experienced agents.
  • FOB, CIF, and CFR pricing options for international contractors.
