Bespoke Design

Corporate Custom Covers

We emboss corporate names, project logos, and utility details into the cover face.

Through composite molding flexibility, we can imprint custom artwork directly onto the surface of gully grates, tank covers, and manhole covers. Whether you require custom color matching to match corporate guidelines, or unique load-bearing properties for specific airport terminal zones, Topaz can develop custom tooling configurations.

Building Components

FRP Window Shades & Doors

Highly weather-resilient building elements for residential and commercial complexes.

- **FRP Window Shades (Chajjas):** Outperforms traditional concrete chajjas that gather moisture, leak, and crack. Lightweight, easy to anchor on external masonry, and pre-pigmented to match external walls.

- **FRP Doors:** Specially designed for high-humidity areas like bathrooms, laboratory clean rooms, and chemical processing zones. Immune to swelling, wrap deformation, rust, or microbial mold.
