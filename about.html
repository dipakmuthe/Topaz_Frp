Production Focus

Advanced Compression Molding

Processing composite matrices under intense compression and high thermal limits.

By heating and compressing reinforcing glass strands within thermoset resin beds, polymers cross-link seamlessly around the reinforcement fibers. This eliminates inner voids and yields high-impact load resilience.
