Our Legacy & Mission

Manufacturing strength and trust in the infrastructure industry since 1986.

Our Foundation

Established Since 1986

Over 35 years of engineering experience and steady distribution of heavy-duty infrastructure assets.

We are proud to refer to more than 35 years of industry experience founded in 1986 in research & development, production, and steady distribution of high-quality products. Originally specializing in mosaic, plain & chequered tiles, interlocking pavers (matt, glossy, and shot blast), concrete blocks,Fly Ash bricks, and precast concrete curb stones, Topaz built an unmatched reputation for product strength.

For more than 20 years, Topaz has been on the approved vendor list in MES (Maharashtra and Other States), Pune Cantonment Board, and the Pune Municipal Corporation (PMC).

"Now, TOPAZ has entered a new market segment known as IROM 2016. Under this division, TOPAZ FRP has become a leading manufacturer and exporter of FRP composite manhole covers, gratings, tank covers, and planters."

Company Spirit

Spirit & Philosophy

Niche for innovation, customer satisfaction, and engineering excellence.

The spirit and philosophy of our fast-paced company is fueled by creating a niche for innovation and creativity in the world of FRP composites. Employees are encouraged to take calculated risks, challenge conventional wisdom, and create innovative FRP products that redefine modern infrastructure.

Within a span of 5 years in the composite segment, TOPAZ FRP has attained complete customer satisfaction and has emerged as one of the prominent names in heavy-duty manhole covers, moulded gratings, window shades, doors, and planters.

Our Own Fleet of Transportation

Guaranteed timely dispatches and site arrivals across India.

Having our own logistics control allows us to provide guaranteed dispatch dates and coordinate closely with site engineers for seamless unloading.
